Brief

Expose studio is wedding and birthday photography studio operating in Chennai. Their team is well known for their Tamil culture who can make the traditional part in a candid style of photography. The best Part in their studio is they Work On clients Budget.

Their requirements

Tenfinish was approached by Aravindhan, the event coordinator for Expose studio. Following are some of the requirements of client.

“I want to show our portfolio in a very appealing way to customers”

“I love taking the shots in portrait as well in landscape. We want the images to be represented on websites in the same way.”

“I want the pricing section to be clear on the website. ”

“The website needs to be colorful”

“Our website should not load as slow as the competitors”

Our approach & outcome

We clearly understood his importance towards representing the images. We had special attention towards images and colorfulness for the entire design process.

Representing images: The client said how the portfolio section wants to be in a detailed manner. From his talk, we understood his importance in the portfolio section. We designed the portfolio section as he requested, in addition, we designed the smooth navigation.

Reduced the load time: The website we were given as reference has high load time. When we analyzed we found that those websites have poor image optimization and images were loading layer by layer. We decided not to have the same to our client. First, we have added preloading screen so all the elements will be loaded properly before showing to a user. Secondly, we have compressed the images given by client as much as possible without losing the quality by various methods. Finally, we have reduced load time.

Reflecting their values: Photography is a competitive field. So we want to reflect our client's values and the way he offer his service to the world. With the help of our in-house content writer, we have projected the expose studio DNA clearly in the about us page.
