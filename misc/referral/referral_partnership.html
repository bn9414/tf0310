Frequently asked questions

Pricing

Is your package negotiable?

Our package is highly flexible based on your affordable budget and requirements, so the negotiation does not make sense here. At the end of any event we offer family membership card, with that you can get discounts for your future events.

What is family membership card?

The family membership card is the identity card for us to know you have used our service before. With this card, we consider you as our family. You get discounts for all your future events as well as to anyone you refer to us with this card too. The discount will be 10% off from the final package price.

How about the advance payment?

For the wedding, as the advance, 30% of the final package price should be paid to confirm the order, and it is enough to pay the next 40% at the end of the event. Finally, the rest 30% should be paid when we deliver the photo books. For other events, 50% of final package price should be paid in advance to confirm the order, then the rest 50% can be paid when we deliver the photo books.

What mode of payments do you accept?

The payment can be done either by Cash or online transaction. Once you book us we will meet you in person to get the advance payment and to collect other details for your event.

Booking

How do I hire your service?

Kindly submit the form in the pricing section, then we will get back to you for discussing your event and price packages. You can also call to book us.

How and when my order gets confirmed?

We design the right packages for your requirements, and you have to choose the one. The Advance amount should be paid to us in order to confirm our service on your event date. Once we receive your payment, the very next moment we confirm your order and avoid the further incoming on the same date.

How many months in advance do we have to book your service?

It is better to book us once your event date gets confirmed so that we can collect necessary details of the event from you and prepare our gears and lighting for your day. If your event is only month away, no issues, feel free to contact us. If we are available on your event date, we can confirm your event. We always do care for customers and put them first.

Photography

What is Candid Wedding Photography?

The simple philosophy of candid photography is keeping things as real as possible because you are real. Do you know when you will be more beautiful? Simple, when being as you, a complete you. Candid photography provides freedom of expression by unlocking you from giving poses in front of a camera. There is no preparation time, there is no time to make you look better in front of lenses, so we freeze your moments as it happens so it is completely natural. This is the way the candid photography is done. We believe that candid photography is the key to cherish your memory for ever.

Do you guys add videography in your package?

Of course, we do. In every package we have videography. To know more please contact us.
