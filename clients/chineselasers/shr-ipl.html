808nm Laser hair removal machine is the process of removing unwanted hair by means of exposure to pulses of laser light 808nm that destroys the hair follicle. The lasers damage the chromospheres by selectively heating them while leaving surrounding tissue unharmed. When treating unwanted hair, for example, the melanin in the hair follicles is targeted and damaged which results in the disruption of hair growth and regeneration. The diode laser is complemented by cooling technology and pain reducing methods which improve treatment efficacy and patient comfort.
