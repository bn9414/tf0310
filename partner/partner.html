Service reseller Partnership

You can use our services in the name of your brand name. You can set your own terms and selling price for our service and outsource your projects to Tenfinish. We always stay anonymous to your clients. On your side you strengthen your contacts and do effective marketing. On our side we take care of development, hiring people, maintain the infrastructure, training the employee to deliver the best service to your clients.

How it works?

1

Add our services under your private label.

2

You take care of marketing, sales, handling the customers till the deal closes (we help you completely from the back end anonymously).

3

After closing the deal we design the website according to the client requirement.

4

You can deliver the completed website under your own brand.

5

Any future changes to the website or the service and maintenance will be done by us under your brand name. As we said, we will be anonymous for the entire journey. We share commission on a mutually agreed basis.

To whom it suits?

For the established companies who wants to extend their services.

Benefits

  • Your business looks great than before because of offering extended services.
  • If your business is like logo designing or digital marketing where clients next step is to choose right web designing company after getting your service, your chance of closing the deal for your services increase because the customer get the web design service from the same company where he does logo design or digital marketing(we mean that whatever the business you do).
