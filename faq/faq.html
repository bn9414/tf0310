Web design

How much advance do I need to pay In order to start the work?

At first, 50% of the final quoted amount should be paid. After completing the project rest of the 50% should be paid. Once we get full amount we make your website live.

How long will you take to develop my website?

The Basic website will be done within 7 working days, Pro will be done in 10 and Premium takes 15 working days to complete. If the requirement of a client is large and complex, the completion time may get extended.

Will I get the revision of the design you make?

Sure. We revise the design until you get satisfied results.

Do I need to pay for service and maintenance?

Yes, 3000/year should be paid for Basic package and 5000/year should be paid for Pro and Premium packages.

After delivering the websites, will you customize the website in future according to my needs?

Most definitely we do. We appoint your future works to the same team who have designed your website. So the customization will be done effectively and efficiently.

What will be the turnaround time to complete the customization?

Usually, it will be done within 3 working days.

Do I need to pay hosting charges separately?

No. You do not want to pay for hosting your website on our servers.

Do I need to provide content?

If it is the Basic package, of course, you have to provide the content. If the package you choose is either Pro or Premium, then we write the content for your website.
