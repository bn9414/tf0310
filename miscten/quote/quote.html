Some common things we do for all clients

Deep-work: No multitasking - We believe in deep work. Once the project is assigned to the team, they work on that alone until completion. By doing so, the team can use their deep cognitive power for your project.

Responsiveness for every pixel: we make your website responsive for every pixel (practical for every 10 pixels). So your website looks great on any devices. Even if a new product between iPhone and iPad is discovered, still your website looks stable and great.

User-centered design: You may be our clients and our work is supposed to satisfy you. But 99.9%, your website is going to be used by your users. So we put more focus on making it comfortable to them.

Technical consultation to increase your business success rate

Your business success is more important to us, so we make everything from content writing to design works that push your business success rate ahead. While we doing the project, we will arrange a discussion with the technical consultant. He will study much about your business nature, client expectation, the gap you have in offline and many other things that impact your business success. After deep understanding of your business, he suggests you essential technical solutions for your business. This can be anything from sending a customized HTML email to adding price calculator in your website. You can keep his suggestion and work on it accordingly. This does not come under the scope of this quotation. You may be charged extra if you are willing to work on his words. This is completely optional one.
